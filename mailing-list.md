We've moved!  All our chatter, announcements, and scheduling now happens on Discord instead of the old Google Groups mailing lists.

Want in?  Ask an existing member for an invite — we'd love to have you.

Once you're in, you'll find channels for Geekend event news, the regular Thursday gaming groups (Dalek and Cylon crews), and general hangout talk.  It's where we coordinate games, share character sheet disasters, and argue about rules interpretations like civilized adventurers.
